Pain is distressing feeling that affects everybody, not just physically but emotionally as well

As Physiotherapists one of our main goals is to heal and relieve the musculo-espequeletical pain. Manual Therapy is a special field of Physiotherapy that uses a combination of techniques, making the pain go away. This is our first step when we treat the our patience

What is Therapeutic exercise?

After taking away the pain, we need to get back on our 100% functionality. Musculoskeletal injuries have the characteristic of reducing and limiting the range of motion, strength and dexterity on the affected part of the body. Therapeutic exercise is not the same kind of exercise that you do in a normal gym. Is a step by step program that allows the patient to recover his functionality and prevent reinjuries.
